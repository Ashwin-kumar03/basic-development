Tips for Financial Cybersecurity

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all financial accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Regularly monitor your bank and credit card statements for unauthorized transactions.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and avoid clicking on suspicious links.
  • Keep your software and antivirus programs up to date.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
  • Secure your mobile devices with passwords or biometric authentication.
  • Educate yourself about common cyber threats and scams.
